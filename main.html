Summary

Dom Cobb is a skilled thief—the absolute best in the dangerous art of extraction, stealing valuable secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb's rare ability has made him a coveted player in corporate espionage but also an international fugitive. He gets a final chance at redemption by performing the impossible—an inception: planting an idea into someone’s mind.
